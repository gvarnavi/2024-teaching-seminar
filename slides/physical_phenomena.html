Takeaway Points​

note

In today's lecture, we covered the following mathematical/physical concepts:

  • Non-dimensionalization allows us to explore the underlying physics
  • Hamilton's equations can be used to derive equations of motion of physical systems
  • The Harmonic approximation is a powerful tool in solving complex problems symbolically
  • More complex problems can be solved numerically
